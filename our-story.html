Our Story

Founded in 2019, OMKAR EXPORTS & IMPORTS has grown from a general export-import business to a leading diamond trader, importer, and exporter known as SHRESTHA DIAMONDS. Our commitment lies in streamlining the diamond trade to efficiently serve the South Indian jewellery market, while also focusing our overseas operations on the diamond and jewellery markets of Nepal.

Our collections are meticulously curated to address the unique preferences of both the South Indian states and Nepal's jewellery sector. Mangaluru acts as our central stock hub, enabling us to fulfil the trading needs of the region and ensuring prompt doorstep delivery within 24–48 hours to any destination in South India. To further strengthen our international presence, we have an overseas facility located in Kathmandu, Nepal, which guarantees a steady and reliable supply of diamonds to Nepal's jewellery industry.

At SHRESTHA DIAMONDS, we are dedicated to supporting jewellers, manufacturers, and designers in selecting diamonds of exceptional quality at highly competitive market prices. Our trading process is designed for complete transparency; clients are provided with samples before any transaction is finalised. Additionally, our representatives personally visit client premises with the necessary samples and, subsequently, the required inventory, ensuring a seamless and trust-based trading experience.
