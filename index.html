Diamond Quality Analysis

Every SHRESTHA DIAMONDS stone is evaluated on five essential quality factors that determine its beauty, value, and authenticity.

Cut

The cut determines brilliance and sparkle. Our diamonds are expertly proportioned for optimal light performance—from Excellent to Good grades.

Color

Graded D (colorless) to Z (light yellow). We specialise in DEF grades for exceptional clarity and premium fancy colours for unique pieces.

Clarity

From Flawless/IF to SI grades. Each stone is assessed for inclusions and blemishes to ensure the finest appearance.

Carat

Weight directly influences size and presence. We offer a full range from 0.20 Ct to 2+ Ct to match every preference.

Certification

Every diamond is certified by GIA, IGI, HRD, or trusted labs, giving you verified authenticity and grading assurance.
