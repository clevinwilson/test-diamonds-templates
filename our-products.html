Our Products

At SHRESTHA DIAMONDS, we are dedicated to offering only the finest diamonds, each diamond follows the Kimberley Process certification. All our diamonds are meticulously cut and polished in renowned Indian facilities, ensuring exceptional craftsmanship and quality.

Every diamond we trade undergoes strict evaluation based on the universally recognised 4Cs—cut, colour, clarity, and carat weight. Each piece is further certified by esteemed institutions such as GIA (Gemological Institute of America) or IGI (International Gemological Institute), providing our clients with added assurance of authenticity and value.

We specialise in supplying a comprehensive selection of premium diamonds, featuring DEF colour grades and clarity classifications such as Flawless/Internally Flawless (F/IF), VVS1, and VVS2. In addition, we provide Syndicate polki diamonds to meet the specific requirements of specialised market segments.

Our general trading portfolio encompasses a wide variety of diamond products, such as -2 stones, Star Melle, solitaires, fancy cuts, fancy-coloured diamonds, and Polki diamonds. This variety ensures that we can meet the unique requirements of jewellers, manufacturers, and designers across the industry.

We guarantee a secure and reliable supply chain by partnering with trusted logistics providers and offering comprehensive insurance coverage for all shipments. Additionally, our services include a 7- day replacement policy under special conditions, ensuring client satisfaction and peace of mind.

To further support our clients, our experienced diamond executives are available to assist jewellers in grading their existing diamond inventory and conducting accurate valuations. We provide complimentary access to our screening machines, which can detect lab-created diamonds and other diamond stimulants.

Other Precious Metals

In addition to our extensive diamond offerings, we are also importers and traders of platinum, rhodium, and palladium. Our involvement in these precious metals allows us to cater to a broader spectrum of clients, supporting the varied needs of jewellers, manufacturers, and designers. By sourcing and trading these sought-after metals, we ensure our partners have access to high-quality materials essential for creating fine jewellery and luxury goods.
