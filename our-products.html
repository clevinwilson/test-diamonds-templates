Welcome To Shrestha
Quality Standards

At SHRESTHA DIAMONDS, we are dedicated to offering only the finest diamonds, each diamond follows the Kimberley Process certification. All our diamonds are meticulously cut and polished in renowned Indian facilities, ensuring exceptional craftsmanship and quality.

Every diamond we trade undergoes strict evaluation based on the universally recognised 4Cs—cut, colour, clarity, and carat weight. Each piece is further certified by esteemed institutions such as GIA (Gemmological Institute of America) or IGI (International Gemmological Institute), providing our clients with added assurance of authenticity and value.

We specialise in supplying a comprehensive selection of premium diamonds, featuring DEF colour grades and clarity classifications such as Flawless/Internally Flawless (F/IF), VVS1, and VVS2. In addition, we provide Syndicate polki diamonds to meet the specific requirements of specialised market segments.
